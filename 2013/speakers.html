Jonathan Maron

The Imaging Source / Senior Web Developer

I have been working in the web application conceptualization and development space since 1996. My current position is with an international company, specialized in the production of industrial machine vision products, application software and software components for developers.

I lead an international development team of programmers, graphical artists, authors and search engine optimizers. Together, we build highly usable, multi-lingual, fully localized web applications that rank well in all search engines for all applicable languages and countries.

Being an advocate of the FOSS movement, I promote the use of Open Source software, motivating and assisting team members to deploy Linux on the desktop and server. I reject the notion of reinventing the wheel and consequently prefer to develop with established frameworks, components and tool kits where applicable.

I am an active member of the Zend Framework, having authored ZendService_LiveDocx, a really exciting project, which offers PHP developers a dead easy and very quick way of creating beautiful word processing documents, such as PDF, DOCX, DOC or RTF files with PHP.

I am also one of the organizers of PHP Unconference Europe (UK) and PHP Unconference Hamburg (Germany).
